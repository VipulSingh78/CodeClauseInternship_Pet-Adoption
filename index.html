Some frequently asked questions.

  • What are the steps to adopt a pet?
    The first step is to come and register for the pet you are wishing to adopt in our nearest centres. We will provide you with a Welcome kit and the details of the pet(the vaccinations done so far, allergies if any e.t.c). And there you go, he/she is all yours.
  • Will any BGC or similar would be done post adoption
    Yes!! Definately there would be a surprise post-adoption check to ensure our pet is happy with their new parents.
  • I will be adopting ,actually would be having a pet for the first time! Would it be feasible.
    Do not Worry at all! Our welcome kit will help you out in all possible way. all you need to do is shower all your love and affection and it will give you all more than that. Happy Adoption
  • Does your NGO accept donations?.
    Yes we do! our organization runs 99% on donations. This encourages us to take the stray pets and give them a better living and we provide a medium for others to adopt and love them more.
  • Where are you located?
    Currently we are located at 3 places: New Delhi, Chennai, Bangalore. We are however looking forward to expand our territory.
